# Getting Started

New to OpenTMS? You're in the right place. This chapter walks you through the very first steps: signing in, the one-time onboarding wizard, and learning how to find your way around the application.

By the end of this chapter you will be able to:

- ✅ Sign in to OpenTMS for the first time
- ✅ Complete the onboarding wizard so the system is ready for daily use
- ✅ Recognize every part of the OpenTMS interface and know what it's for
- ✅ Manage your own account: profile, password, language and notifications

## What's in this chapter

1. **[Logging in](./logging-in)** — opening OpenTMS in your browser and signing in (with or without single sign-on).
2. **[The onboarding wizard](./onboarding-wizard)** — the first-run wizard that prepares your tenant.
3. **[Navigating OpenTMS](./navigating/overview)** — the app bar, the user menu, notifications and chat.

:::tip Already signed in?
If you already use OpenTMS day-to-day and you're looking for help on a specific module, you can skip this chapter and jump straight to **[Planboard](../planboard/overview)** or **[Transport](../transport/overview)**.
:::
