# The app bar

The **app bar** is the strip across the top of every OpenTMS screen. It's your launchpad: from here you switch between modules, search, see what needs your attention, and manage your own account.

<figure>

![App menu showing module tiles — placeholder](./img/02-app-menu.png)

<figcaption>Clicking the app menu in the app bar opens the launcher with all available modules.</figcaption>
</figure>

## What's on the app bar

| Element | What it does |
|---|---|
| **OpenTMS logo** | Click to return to the **Dashboard**. |
| **App menu** | Opens the full grid of modules. See [The app menu](#the-app-menu) below. |
| **Search** | Quick search across the current module. |
| **Notifications bell** | Recent system notifications. See [Notifications](./notifications). |
| **Chat icon** | The in-app chat. A red badge shows unread messages. See [Chat](./chat). |
| **Language switcher** | Change the interface language on the fly. |
| **User menu** | Your profile picture or initials. See [Your user menu](./user-menu). |
| **Tenant indicator** | Shows your current tenant in the format `Tenant\Username`. |

If your account has been **impersonated** by an administrator (for support), a small red icon appears in the app bar. Click it to return to your own session.

## The app menu

The app menu is the central launcher for all modules in OpenTMS. Open it by clicking the **app menu icon** in the app bar (a grid of dots).

The app menu groups apps into three sections:

| Section | Contains |
|---|---|
| **Standard apps** | The core OpenTMS modules: Dashboard, Planboard, Transport, Finance, Track & Trace and more. |
| **FiLogic & Friends** | Apps from FiLogic partners that have been enabled for your tenant. |
| **Private apps** | Custom apps built specifically for your organization. |

You can:

- **Search** for an app by name using the search bar at the top of the menu.
- **Reorder** apps by dragging them — the order is saved per user.
- See **recently used** apps highlighted at the top.

Click any tile to open that module. You can have multiple browser tabs open in different modules at the same time.

## The module menu (sidebar)

Below the app bar on the left side of the screen sits the **module menu** — the list of pages for the **module you are currently in**. The module menu changes when you switch modules: in Planboard it shows planning items, in Transport it shows transport-related items, and so on.

- Click any item to open it.
- Items can be nested up to four levels deep — click a parent to expand it.
- Items you don't have permission for are hidden automatically.
- You can collapse the menu with the arrow at its top or bottom for more screen real estate.

:::tip
The fastest way to get from one module to another is **app menu → click tile**. The module menu is for navigating *within* a module.
:::
