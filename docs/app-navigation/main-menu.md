# The main menu

The **main menu** is the side menu on the left of every OpenTMS screen. It always shows the navigation items for the **module you are currently in**.

<figure>

![Side menu expanded — placeholder](./img/05-side-menu.png)

<figcaption>The main menu adapts to the active module.</figcaption>
</figure>

## How it behaves

- **Module-aware** — the items in the side menu change automatically when you open a different module from the [app menu](./app-shell#the-app-menu).
- **Permission-aware** — items you cannot access are hidden, not greyed out. If a colleague's menu looks different from yours, that is why.
- **Hierarchical** — items can have sub-items up to four levels deep. Click a parent item to expand or collapse it. The currently active item is highlighted.

## Collapsing the menu

You can collapse the side menu to leave more space for your content area:

1. Click the **collapse arrow** at the top or bottom of the side menu.
2. The menu shrinks to a column of icons. Hover over an icon to see the label as a tooltip.
3. Click the arrow again to expand the menu back.

Your collapse preference is saved per user.

## Tips

:::tip
The fastest way to get from one module to another is **app menu → click tile**. The side menu is for navigating *within* a module.
:::

:::tip
If a menu item you expect is missing, check with your administrator — it is almost always a permission, not a bug.
:::
