# Planboard

The **Planboard** is the central workspace for planners and dispatchers in OpenTMS. From here you can see all transport files, assign them to vehicles, plan trips on a timeline, monitor stops and legs, and keep an eye on your fleet on a map — all in one screen.

![Planboard full view — placeholder](./img/01-planboard-full.png)

<figcaption>The Planboard combines multiple views into a single, configurable workspace.</figcaption>
</figure>

## Who is the Planboard for?

The Planboard is used mainly by:

- **Planners** who assign freight to vehicles and drivers.
- **Dispatchers** who follow the execution of trips during the day.
- **Operations leads** who need a quick overview of what is going on in the fleet.

You need the permission **Applications → OpenTmsApp → Planboard app** to open the Planboard. Individual tabs (like the Vehicle Planboard) require their own permissions — see [Layout & tabs](./layout-and-tabs#permissions).

## Open the Planboard

1. Click the **app menu** icon in the header bar (the grid of dots).
2. In the **Standard apps** section, click **Planboard**.

OpenTMS opens the Planboard in the content area. The first time you open it, OpenTMS loads a **default layout** with a few tabs visible. You can customize this — see [Layout & tabs](./layout-and-tabs).
