# Welcome to OpenTMS

OpenTMS is a Transport Management System built by [FiLogic](https://filogic.nl) for transport and logistics companies. It brings together planning, dispatch, documentation, tracking and invoicing in one connected environment.

This documentation is the user guide. It is written for **dispatchers, planners, drivers and office staff** who use OpenTMS every day — not for administrators or developers.

## How to read this guide

Each chapter starts with a short **overview** of what the screen is for, followed by **step-by-step tasks** ("How do I...?") and ends with a **reference** of buttons and fields.

- Buttons and menu items are shown in **bold**.
- Screens and sections are shown in _italics_.
- Keyboard shortcuts look like this: <kbd>Ctrl</kbd> + <kbd>S</kbd>.
